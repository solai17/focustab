Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2025

Overview

ByteLetters ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our Chrome extension and related services.

Information We Collect

When you use ByteLetters, we collect the following information:

  • Email address — Collected via Google Sign-In for account authentication
  • Newsletter content — Emails you forward to your ByteLetters inbox for processing
  • User preferences — Your settings and saved bytes

How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely to provide and improve ByteLetters:

  • Authenticate your account and enable sign-in
  • Process forwarded newsletters to extract insights
  • Sync your bytes and preferences across devices
  • Generate your unique newsletter forwarding address

What We Don't Do

We respect your privacy and commit to the following:

  • We do not sell your personal data to third parties
  • We do not track your browsing activity outside the extension
  • We do not use advertising or analytics trackers
  • We do not share your newsletter content with anyone
  • We do not use your data for AI model training

AI Processing

Your newsletters are processed using AI services (Google Gemini and Anthropic Claude) to extract insights. This processing is done solely to provide the ByteLetters service. Your newsletter content is not stored by these AI providers and is not used to train their models.

Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely:

  • Data is stored on Supabase (PostgreSQL) with encryption at rest
  • All connections use HTTPS encryption
  • Authentication is handled via Google OAuth 2.0
  • We follow industry-standard security practices

Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. Newsletter content is processed and then the raw content is deleted; only the extracted bytes are stored.

Your Rights & Data Deletion

You have full control over your data:

  • You can view all your saved bytes in the extension
  • You can delete individual bytes at any time
  • You can delete your entire account and all associated data from the extension settings

To delete your account: Open ByteLetters → Settings (gear icon) → Delete Account

Third-Party Services

ByteLetters uses the following third-party services:

  • Google OAuth — For authentication
  • Supabase — For data storage
  • Google Gemini — For AI text processing
  • Anthropic Claude — For AI text processing (fallback)

Children's Privacy

ByteLetters is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of any material changes by updating the "Last updated" date and, for significant changes, by email.
